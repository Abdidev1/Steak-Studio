Our Story

A Cut Above
the Rest

Steak Studio on Canal Bank Road is Lahore's destination for premium steakhouse dining. Set along the iconic canal, our Canal Branch has become a beloved landmark for food enthusiasts seeking quality, ambiance, and an unforgettable culinary experience.

From sizzling steaks to lavish buffets, hi-tea spreads, and Iftar feasts — every visit is a distinct occasion. Our team takes pride in warm hospitality, a refined atmosphere, and dishes crafted with care and consistency.
